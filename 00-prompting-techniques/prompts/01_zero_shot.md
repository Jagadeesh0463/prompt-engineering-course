# 01 — Zero-shot Prompting Output

## Task
Summarization (Cricket)

## Why This Is Zero-shot
This prompt gives a clear instruction without providing any examples.
The model relies purely on its pre-trained knowledge to complete the task.


## Prompt Used
Summarize the following cricket match report into 4 simple bullet points:

India scored 285 runs batting first. Virat Kohli scored 120 runs.
Australia started strong but lost quick wickets in middle overs.
Indian bowlers maintained pressure and won the match by 35 runs.


## Model Response
Here are 4 simple bullet points summarizing the match report:

- India batted first and scored 285 runs, with Virat Kohli scoring 120.
- Australia started their chase strongly but then lost quick wickets in the middle overs.
- Indian bowlers maintained consistent pressure on the Australian batsmen.
- India won the match by 35 runs.
